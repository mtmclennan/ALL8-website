## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device to store information. We use:

- **Strictly necessary** cookies — required for site functionality and security.
- **Analytics** cookies — to understand usage and improve the Site.
- **Advertising** cookies — to deliver and measure ads (if enabled/consented).

## Your Choices

You can manage cookies in our on‑site banner and in your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some features may not work.

## Specific Technologies
